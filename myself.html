Things Which I Likes

Cinema

I like cinema because it always teaches me something new about different aspects of life.

Cinema is more than entertainment for me.

It always enriches me.

Cricket

Cricket is a game, but not just a game.

It teaches you many things.

For example, it teaches patience. If you are looking for results too quickly, cricket reminds you that they won’t always come right away.

During that time, it teaches you to remain patient.

It also teaches you that some things take time to happen.

Gym

No matter what I am going through in my life, going to the gym helps me forget all the worries running through my mind.

The gym makes me feel refreshed and light.

For me, the gym is not just a place — it is like a friend.

Interviews

I started watching interviews regularly over the last two years.

I enjoy watching interviews because I learn from the experiences of the people being interviewed.

Listening to their stories and journeys has given me new perspectives about life.
